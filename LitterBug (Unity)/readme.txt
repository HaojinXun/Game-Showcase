
Game Title: LitterBug

Environment: Unity

Date: 2022 June-September

The game was developed with Scrum principles in a group of five students at Carleton University.

Haojin Xun was mainly responsible for the overall game design, mechanics of different equipment (e.g., Vacuum cleaner), environmental events (e.g., Cars, Vending machines), and AI (e.g., the RatKing Boss Mechanics).
